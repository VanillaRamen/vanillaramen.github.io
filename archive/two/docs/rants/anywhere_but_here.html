anywhere but here

12 may 2025

i wish i were anywhere but here

i wish i was in a nyc hotel for 48 hours
a high-rise with balconies and windows
one right by my desk
so i could watch the people while i take care of things

i wish i could lie in bed all day
and watch anime i've wanted to watch
and read manga i've been meaning to read
and finally let go of the things that have brought me down

i wish i could get away from it all
the stress and anxiety
the life or death
the finals

is being an engineer worth it
if every day i get closer
to thinking of ending it all
if i never reach my first design

what would it all have been for
if i end up throwing it all away
an untimely end
to something that just began

what would it all have been for

i wish i were anywhere but here
