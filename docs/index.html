This is my personal domain on the internet. I made this for myself, but I hope you can enjoy it too.

Unfortunately, this isn't a website that hosts anything super impressive, like beautiful photographs or gorgeous illustrations (for now). Instead, it's somewhere for me to escape to, building a shelter for my brain brick by brick.

> About Me

It's not a personal website without a little personal information, is it?

I'm a computer engineering major at a university known for engineering but not so known that I expect you to know it. At the time of writing (or last updating this text), I'm about to enter my second year of studies in university.

I'm mainly hoping to be able to work closely with computer architecture and build something I can be proud of. And I especially hope ASM treats me well after a year of working with C++.

> Aspirations

This is an unreasonable goal, but I think one day I would love to have a keyboard that I made entirely from scratch, down to the computer chip handling all of the logic and protocols (except connectivity like Bluetooth and USB).

I know I could just use an existing programmable little computer thing like an Arduino or an ESP32, but wouldn't it be so cool to say that I designed a little chip myself? And it's not like I would need that much computational power.
