my stance on artificial intelligence

this site was made by me using my human hands and my human brain; it won't ever be created – neither in part nor in whole – with the use of artificial intelligence

i think artificial intelligence has a place in research and certain day-to-day cases as a pattern-recognition tool. for instance, it can be used to assist medical professionals in diagnosing certain conditions, or it can be used to assist researchers looking at complex proteins, or it can be used to assist linguists who are analyzing the semantics of language. i think all these and similar use cases are very, very valid

however, i don't think artificial intelligence should be used in many of the ways it's currently used: to compose (especially important) emails; to generate code – especially code that is incredibly difficult to debug or understand, and especially code that gets used in publicly/commercially available programs and services (microsoft windows); to hallucinate misinformation and act as an all-agreeing yes-man to some of the most vulnerable of society (chatbots); to infringe on the rights of others (copyright, sexual consent law, etc.); etc.

tl;dr: artificial intelligence shouldn't be used to wastefully and lazily create something mediocre; artificial intelligence shouldn't be used to harm others
